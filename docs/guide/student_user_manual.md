## 1. Getting Started

### 1.1 Creating Your Account

**Step 1: Open NEXUS**

- Visit: `https://nexus.school.lk`
- Or open the NEXUS app on your phone

**Step 2: Click "Register"**

**Step 3: Fill in Your Information**

```
✓ First Name: Your first name
✓ Last Name: Your last name
✓ Email: Your school email (e.g., john.doe@student.school.lk)
✓ Password: At least 8 characters, 1 uppercase, 1 lowercase, 1 number
✓ Admission Number: Your student ID (e.g., STU2024001)
✓ Grade: Select your grade (1-13)
✓ Class Section: Your class (e.g., 10A)
```

**Step 4: Verify Your Email**

- Check your email inbox
- Click the verification link
- Your account is now active!

### 1.2 Logging In

1. Go to `https://nexus.school.lk`
2. Click **"Sign In"**
3. Enter your email and password
4. Click **"Sign In"**

**💡 Tip:** Check "Remember Me" to stay logged in on your device

### 1.3 Your Library Card

After logging in, you'll receive a digital library card with a QR code.

**To view your card:**

1. Click your profile icon (top right)
2. Select **"My Library Card"**
3. Show this QR code to the librarian when borrowing books

**📱 Pro Tip:** Save your library card to your phone's home screen for quick access!

## 3. Borrowing Books

### 3.1 Can I Borrow This Book?

**Check Availability:**

1. Find the book (search or browse)
2. Look at the status badge:
    - 🟢 **Available** - You can borrow it now!
    - 🔴 **Checked Out** - Currently borrowed
    - 🟡 **Reserved** - Someone has reserved it

**Your Borrowing Limits:**

- Maximum: **3 books** at a time
- Loan duration: **14 days**
- Renewals allowed: **Up to 2 times** (if not reserved)

### 3.2 How to Borrow

**Online (Check availability):**

1. Find the book you want
2. Click on the book
3. See if it's available
4. Go to the library with your library card

**At the Library:**

1. Take the book to the librarian's desk
2. Show your digital library card (QR code)
3. Librarian will scan the book and your card
4. Done! Book is now checked out to you

**📧 You'll receive a confirmation email with:**

- Book title
- Due date
- Renewal options

### 3.3 Due Dates

**Important Information:**

- Books are due **14 days** after checkout
- You'll get a reminder **2 days before** due date
- Return on time to avoid fines!

**Check Your Due Dates:**

1. Go to **"My Loans"**
2. See all borrowed books
3. Due dates are clearly shown

**Color Coding:**

- 🟢 Green: 5+ days remaining
- 🟡 Yellow: 2-4 days remaining
- 🔴 Red: Overdue

## 5. Reservations

### 5.1 What is a Reservation?

When a book you want is checked out, you can **reserve** it. You'll be notified when it's available for you to borrow.

**How it works:**

- You join a queue (line)
- When the book is returned, the first person in queue is notified
- You have **24 hours** to claim the book

### 5.2 Making a Reservation

**Step-by-Step:**

1. Find the book (it shows "Checked Out")
2. Click on the book
3. Click **"Reserve This Book"**
4. Confirm reservation

**You'll see:**

- Your position in the queue (e.g., "Position: 3")
- Estimated wait time
- Other people also waiting

**Reservation Limits:**

- Maximum: **5 active reservations**
- Free of charge
- Can cancel anytime

### 5.3 When Your Book is Ready

**You'll be notified:**

- 📧 Email
- 📱 Push notification (if enabled)
- 🔔 In-app notification

**What to do:**

1. You have **24 hours** to pick it up
2. Go to the library
3. Show your library card
4. Borrow the book normally

**⚠️ Important:**

- If you don't claim within 24 hours, reservation expires
- The book goes to the next person in queue
- You won't lose your place for other reservations

### 5.4 Managing Reservations

**View Your Reservations:**

1. Click **"Reservations"** in menu
2. See all active reservations

**For each reservation:**

- Book details
- Queue position
- Status (Pending / Available)
- Estimated wait time (if pending)
- Claim deadline (if available)

**Cancel a Reservation:**

1. Go to **"Reservations"**
2. Find the reservation
3. Click **"Cancel"**
4. Confirm cancellation

## 6. Fines & Payments

### 6.1 Understanding Fines

**Why fines exist:**

- Encourage on-time returns
- Ensure books are available for everyone

**Fine Rates:**

- **Late return:** Rs. 5.00 per day
- **Maximum fine per book:** Rs. 500.00
- **Damaged book:** 50% of book price
- **Lost book:** 100% of book price + Rs. 100 processing

### 6.2 Viewing Your Fines

**Check your fine balance:**

1. Go to **"My Fines"**
2. See all fines (pending and paid)

**Each fine shows:**

- Book title
- Fine type (Overdue, Damage, Lost)
- Amount
- Days overdue
- Status (Pending, Paid, Waived)

**💡 Quick View:**

- Your total fine balance is shown in the top menu
- Red badge if you have pending fines

### 6.3 Payment Restrictions

**If your fines exceed Rs. 100:**

- ❌ Cannot borrow new books
- ❌ Cannot renew current loans
- ✅ Can still reserve books
- ✅ Can use Digital Vault

**To resume borrowing:**

- Pay your fines at the library

### 6.4 Paying Fines

**At the Library:**

1. Go to the librarian's desk
2. Say you want to pay fines
3. Show your library card
4. Pay in cash or bank transfer
5. Get a receipt

**After Payment:**

- Fines marked as "Paid" immediately
- You can borrow books again
- Receipt sent to your email

### 6.5 Fine Waivers

In special circumstances, fines can be waived:

- First-time late return
- System errors
- School closures
- Medical emergencies

**How to request:**

1. Visit the library
2. Speak with the librarian
3. Explain the situation
4. Librarian will forward to admin

**📝 Note:** Waiver decisions are at admin discretion

## 10. Troubleshooting

### 10.1 Login Issues

**Problem: Forgot Password**

1. Click **"Forgot Password?"** on login page
2. Enter your email
3. Check email for reset link
4. Click link and create new password

**Problem: Account Locked**

- After 5 failed login attempts
- Wait 15 minutes
- Or contact librarian to unlock

**Problem: Email Not Verified**

1. Check spam/junk folder
2. Click **"Resend Verification Email"**
3. Check inbox again
4. Click verification link

### 10.2 Borrowing Issues

**Problem: Can't Borrow Books**

**Possible Reasons:**

1. **At loan limit (3 books)** → Return a book first
2. **Fines over Rs. 100** → Pay fines at library
3. **Overdue books** → Return overdue books
4. **Account suspended** → Contact administration

**Problem: Renewal Button Disabled**

**Reasons:**

1. Book is reserved by someone else
2. Maximum renewals reached (2)
3. Book is overdue
4. You have unpaid fines over Rs. 100

### 10.3 Reservation Issues

**Problem: Can't Reserve Book**

**Possible Reasons:**

1. **At reservation limit (5)** → Cancel an existing reservation
2. **Book is available** → Borrow it directly instead
3. **Already reserved this book** → Check "My Reservations"

**Problem: Missed Claim Window**

- Reservation expired after 24 hours
- Book went to next person in queue
- Make a new reservation if still needed

### 10.4 Technical Issues

**Problem: Page Won't Load**

1. Check internet connection
2. Refresh the page (F5 or Cmd+R)
3. Clear browser cache
4. Try a different browser

**Problem: App Not Working**

1. Close and reopen app
2. Check for app updates
3. Restart your phone
4. Reinstall app if needed

**Problem: Notifications Not Received**

1. Check notification settings (in profile)
2. Check email spam folder
3. Enable notifications in phone settings
4. Contact librarian if issue persists

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I borrow books for my friends?** A: No, each student must use their own library card. Books are tracked to your account.

**Q: What if I lose a book?** A: Immediately inform the librarian. You'll be charged the book price + Rs. 100 processing fee.

**Q: Can I return books early?** A: Yes! There's no penalty for early returns.

**Q: Do e-books count toward my 3-book limit?** A: No, Digital Vault resources are unlimited and free.

**Q: How do I know if a book is good?** A: Check the ratings and reviews on the book's page. Also see "Trending in Grade X" for popular choices.

**Q: Can I suggest books for the library to buy?** A: Yes! Use the "Suggest a Book" form (bottom of Books page) or speak with the librarian.

**Q: What happens to my account when I graduate?** A: Your account status changes to "Graduated" but you can still access the Digital Vault.

**Q: Is my reading history private?** A: Yes, only you can see your reading history. Leaderboard participation is opt-in only.

## Glossary

**Accession Number:** Unique ID assigned to each physical book copy

**Due Date:** Last day to return a book without a fine

**Fine:** Penalty charged for late returns or damage

**Loan:** The period when you have borrowed a book

**Queue:** Line of people waiting to borrow a checked-out book

**Renewal:** Extending your loan period by 14 more days

**Reservation:** Placing yourself in queue for a checked-out book

**Streak:** Consecutive days with at least one active loan

**Digital Vault:** Collection of free downloadable study materials
